# Privacy Policy - Crosshair F

**Last updated:** April 21, 2026
**Effective date:** April 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Crosshair F ("the App", "we", "us") handles user data.

## Summary (TL;DR)

Crosshair F does **NOT** collect, track, or transmit any personal information about you. The app is fully offline by default. The only optional online feature is the Community tab, which you control entirely.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **NOT** collect your real name
- We do **NOT** collect your email address
- We do **NOT** collect your IP address (beyond temporary server logs for spam prevention)
- We do **NOT** track which games you play
- We do **NOT** capture screenshots or screen recordings
- We do **NOT** read keystrokes or mouse input
- We do **NOT** read your game memory
- We do **NOT** use telemetry or analytics tools (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
- We do **NOT** sell, rent, or share any data with advertisers

## Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored on **your device only**, in a local config file (`%APPDATA%/crosshair-f-config`):

- Your crosshair settings (shape, color, size, position)
- Saved game profiles you create
- Hotkey bindings
- Whether you accepted the disclaimer
- Last-selected display monitor

This data never leaves your computer. You can delete it anytime by removing the `%APPDATA%/Crosshair F` folder.

## Information Sent to Our Servers (Community Feature Only)

The Community feature is **optional**. When you choose to upload a crosshair to the community, we send the following to our backend (hosted on Supabase):

- The crosshair preset data (shape, colors, numeric settings as JSON)
- The display name you typed (you choose what to enter, no real name needed)
- The game category you selected
- Tags and description you typed
- Timestamp of upload

When you browse community crosshairs, your computer fetches the public list from our server.

We do **not** log identifying information about visitors beyond standard server logs (IP address, retained 30 days for spam prevention only, then deleted automatically).

## Third-Party Services

- **Supabase** (database for community feature) - Privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
- **VirusTotal** (optional, for scanning user-uploaded images) - Privacy policy: https://docs.virustotal.com/docs/personal-data-policy

## Children's Privacy

Crosshair F is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has uploaded data through our community feature, contact us and we will delete it.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete all local data by uninstalling the app and removing `%APPDATA%/Crosshair F`
- Request deletion of any community upload by reporting it from the app
- Contact us with any privacy questions

## Data Retention

- Local data: stays until you delete it
- Community uploads: stay until you or a moderator removes them
- Server logs: deleted after 30 days

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated through an in-app notice. Continued use after changes means acceptance of the new policy.
