Crosshair F Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

Crosshair F does not collect any personal information. The app works entirely on your device.

Community feature

When you upload a crosshair or game position to the community, we store:

  • The crosshair settings you chose to share
  • A tag name you chose (optional)
  • An anonymous device identifier (so you can update/delete your own uploads)

We do not collect your name, email, IP address, or any other personal data.

Reviews and ratings

When you rate or review a community item, we store your rating, optional text, and the same anonymous device identifier. Reviews are public and visible to other users.

Data storage

Community data is stored on Supabase (EU servers). Local app settings are stored on your Windows device only.

Data retention

Community uploads and reviews remain stored until you delete them from the app or request removal. Local settings on your device remain until you uninstall the app or manually clear them.

Your rights

You can at any time:

  • Delete any crosshair or game position you uploaded directly from the app
  • Delete any review you submitted by submitting a new one (it overwrites the old)
  • Request full removal of all your data by emailing us
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data from your device

Children's privacy

Crosshair F does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Because we do not collect names, emails, or identifying personal data from anyone, the app is safe for users of any age. If you believe a child has uploaded content that should be removed, contact us and we will remove it.

Third-party services

We use Supabase (supabase.com) to host the community backend. When the community feature is used, anonymous request data (the settings you share and an anonymous device ID) is sent to Supabase servers. Supabase has its own privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date.
